Our Mission

We are on a mission to bridge the gap between
education and impact.

We design, deliver, and scale Challenge-Based Learning experiences that connect Europe's leading universities, corporations, and public-sector leaders.

From EIT to Global Impact

Kimitisik was founded by learners who experienced firsthand the power of applied innovation. They saw a critical need to connect academic research with real-world entrepreneurial action.

Today, our team of academics, engineers, and expert facilitators operates at the intersection of education and industry, empowering people to create tangible impact. We don't just consult; we co-create as peers who understand the rigor of academia and the pace of industry.

Did you know?
